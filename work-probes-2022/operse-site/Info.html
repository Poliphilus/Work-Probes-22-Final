About Us :

Welcome to Operse! We are a unique web development company, a two man operation, and everything you see on our portfolio is done from scratch. Yes, that's right- no WIX/Weebly or even Squarespace. But enough on how great we are, let's dive into us and who we really are!

Operse started during the first Covid pandemic lockdown. Being based in Cork, we saw huge job losses in the area which affected us personally here at Operse. Both of our members had been working in the hospitality industry, and this put them in a sticky situation. But, instead of sitting down and allowing it to consume us, we decided to refresh and develop with skills we acquired through our own passion for developing and our formal education. With this in mind we set out for a name.

During this time, the first lockdown had ended and we went back to our normal lives (as normal as possible). Our budding enterprise, still without a name at this point, was pushed back on hold for a moment. But it truly was for just a moment, as the second lockdown was looming on us all! We were hit hard again with another very strict Level 5 lockdown, which saw jobs being lost again, along with us here at Operse.

From that point we knew it was a sign to get into the Web Development business. We have seen a lot of people online are offering this service called “web design”. Now, we don’t hate or judge anybody who is trying to earn a living, but we have seen people out there charging upwards of €1,500 for a Wix designed website, with added SEO marketing and all that jazz. You might think “OMG!! That is amazing!”, but let us explain to you why it's not.

Web Design and Web Development are two of the same, however, as stated above, here at Operse we pride ourselves on building something from zero-to-hero. This means we take a blank HTML canvas and build your site, then move onto CSS to style your website's theme. We finally move to JavaScript, the language that allows us to make these amazing animations/tools come to life! See, we charge a rate which we can 100% guarantee can't be found anywhere else online. We know this for a fact, as we design them exactly to our customers taste and needs, but also with the consumer in mind.

Thanks for reading and visiting the about us section. We hope to hear more from you soon, either as an interested developer or a customer!

Regards,
Operse Team.
